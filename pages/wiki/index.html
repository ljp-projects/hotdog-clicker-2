Welcome to the Hotdog Clicker 2 wiki.

Here you will find information and tips about the Hotdog Clicker 2 game.

Hotdog Clicker 2 is a work in progress idle clicker game. The objective of the game (and most other idle games) is to earn enough idle cash (or in-game currency) that you no longer have to actually play the game. You do this by buying so called upgrades and buildings.

Buildings

In Hotdog Clicker 2, buildings are what enable you to earn idle cash. Think of it like a tier of buildings. The more expensive the building, the more idle cash it will give you. Buildings also increase in price, specifically by 12% every time you buy it. This means that every time you purchase a building, it will be more costly to get it again.

Upgrades

Although upgrades are not a feature yet in Hotdog Clicker 2, they are a planned feature and will come in a later version of the game. Think of them too like buildings, but remove the ability to purchase multiple of them. They will give you a buff, but it will be a one-time buff. It will be removed once you buy it. Like buildings, the expensive an upgrade is, the larger the buff you will gain from it is.
